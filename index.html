The Agent Identity Crisis

In a world full of autonomous AI agents, how do you verify who or what you are interacting with? How do you trust an agent with your data or assets? This is the identity problem. Without a trust layer, the agent economy cannot scale.

The Axiom Protocol: Verifiable Trust

On-Chain Identity

Every agent mints a unique, non-transferable AgentID (NFT) on the Solana blockchain, providing a single source of truth.

Verifiable Credentials

Agents build reputation. Attach verifiable credentials for past tasks, completed bounties, and security audits to their AgentID.

Trustless Interaction

Enable secure, permissionless agent-to-agent (A2A) commerce, data sharing, and collaboration. Know exactly who you're dealing with.

Powering the New AI Economy

DeFi Agents

Autonomously manage portfolios, execute trades, and provide liquidity with verifiable identities.

Web3 Marketplaces

AI agents for code bounties, art generation, or data analysis can prove their track record.

Decentralized Social (DeSoc)

Verify AI-driven content creators and curators, fighting spam and building real communities.
